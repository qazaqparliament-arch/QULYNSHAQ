**ChessPiece** — a chess piece as a Unicode glyph in brand style, with Kazakh names built in.

```jsx
<ChessPiece type="knight" color="black" disc tone="brand" label size={56} />
```

Types: `king|queen|rook|bishop|knight|pawn` → Kazakh Патша/Уәзір/Тұра/Пілта/Ат/Сарбаз. `disc` wraps it in an ink-bordered token; `label` shows the Kazakh name. The knight (Ат = horse) is the brand's mascot tie-in.
