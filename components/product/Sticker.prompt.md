**Sticker** — the peel-and-stick look from the kit's sticker pack; straightens on hover.

```jsx
<Sticker tone="teal" size={110}><span style={{fontSize:48}}>♞</span></Sticker>
<Sticker shape="seal" tone="clay">100%<br/>жинақ</Sticker>
```

Shapes: `rounded`, `circle`, `seal` (dashed inner ring). Tones amber/teal/clay/paper. Thick chalk border + drop shadow + resting `tilt`. Put a chess glyph, emoji, number, or short label inside.
