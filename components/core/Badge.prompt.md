**Badge** — small label pill for lesson numbers, difficulty, and status.

```jsx
<Badge tone="clay" variant="solid">Сабақ 03</Badge>
<Badge tone="olive" iconLeft={<span>✓</span>}>Оңай</Badge>
```

Tones: `brand` (amber), `teal`, `clay`, `olive`, `neutral`, `ink`. Variants: `soft` (default), `solid`, `outline`. Sizes `sm|md`.
